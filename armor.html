Armor Sets and Protective Wear

The following list of items is an assortment of wear and cover to protect you from your foes. The list below is what I currently have in stock. Each item has been thoroughly inspected to ensure quality of protection. Your safety is of the utmost priority to my services. I look forward to seeing this equipment on you or your party members!

Briarcoat

Plate Mail

+2 Defense

-2 Blunt Damage, -2 Sharp Damage

Traded from the Dwarves in Golddepth Mountains, this plate covering is sleek, sturdy, and imposing. Your troops and warriors will be able to charge into battle with the courage of dwarven steel at the ready!

Armor of the Holy Order

Light Plate

+1 Defense

-1 Blunt Damage, -1 Sharp Damage, -5 Unholy Damage

This armor was a common sight among the local religious order of Friarsted. During an outbreak of undead by the hands of the Lich-lord Cra'mel, the priests of the sect blessed a multitude of equipment to shield their defenders against Cra'mel's curses and spells.

After the crisis was overcome, the priests continued to provide such holy protection for anyone willing to support their monastaries, either through faith or coin alike.

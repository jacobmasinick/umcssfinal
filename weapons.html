Frostfang

Bastard Sword / "Hand-and-a-half" Sword

+1 Attack

1d8 + 2 Cold Damage

A large sword used by the half-giants of Jurthok, this blade was imbued with runes by their chieftan. While used in only one hand by their native warriors, it is heavy enough that it needs to hands to use effectively without proper strength. It can deal swift and powerful frozen strikes to your enemies.

The Jurthok warriors, understanding my requests for a smaller item to provide, taught me the basic runes to "cold wrap" other items.
